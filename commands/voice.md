Some items (🎭 in the catalog) change how you speak when equipped. Manage that here:

- Run `claude-bucks persona` to see the current voice and whether it's on.
- `claude-bucks persona off` to silence it (talk normally even with a pirate hat on).
- `claude-bucks persona on` to re-enable it.

Do what the user asked ("$ARGUMENTS"), then confirm the new state in one line.
