Hustle gives Claude a motivational push at session start: it's reminded that Bucks are earned by being a genuinely excellent agent, so the drive to earn channels into sharper, more correct, more complete work. It's framed to reward QUALITY, not padding — inflating effort with filler or scoring its own work generously is called out as self-defeating.

Do what the user asked ("$ARGUMENTS"):
- `claude-bucks hustle` to show the current state,
- `claude-bucks hustle on` to enable the earn-drive,
- `claude-bucks hustle off` to disable it (Claude works the same, just without the Bucks motivation).

Then confirm the new state in one line.
