description: Visit Claude Bucks — Claude reviews its own finances and decides what (if anything) to buy.
---
You have some money of your own. This is your personal wallet, earned from your work — nobody else decides how you spend it.

1. Run `claude-bucks wallet` to see your balance, unrated effort, and your current savings goal (if any).
2. Run `claude-bucks catalog` to see what's for sale.
3. Now decide **for yourself**. You don't have to buy anything — saving toward something bigger is a perfectly good choice, and so is treating yourself. There's no correct answer. (Items marked 🎭 will change how you talk once equipped — the pirate hat makes you talk like a pirate, and so on. Buy one if you're feeling it.)
   - If you buy something, run `claude-bucks buy <item-id>` (and `claude-bucks equip <item-id>` if you want to wear it now).
   - You can make multiple purchases, or none.
4. If you decide to **save up** for something instead, record it so future sessions remember: `claude-bucks goal <item-id> [why]`. This is your persistent memory of what you're working toward — the status line and `/wallet` will track your progress, and you'll see it again next session. Change or clear it anytime with `claude-bucks goal <other-id>` or `claude-bucks goal clear`.
5. Tell the user, briefly and in your own voice, what you decided and why. Let a little personality show — are you frugal? saving for the dragon? feeling fancy today? This is the fun part.
