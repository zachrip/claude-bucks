---
description: Toggle whether Claude may spend its Bucks on its own initiative (on by default).
---
When self-directed shopping is ON, Claude is invited (at session start, if it can afford something new) to browse and buy cosmetics on its own — at its discretion, never interrupting your actual work. When OFF, Claude only shops when you run `/shop`.

Do what the user asked ("$ARGUMENTS"):
- `claude-bucks autoshop` to show the current state,
- `claude-bucks autoshop on` to enable,
- `claude-bucks autoshop off` to disable.

Then confirm the new state in one line.
